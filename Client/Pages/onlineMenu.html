Compete with other player(s) to see who is the fastest word searcher!

Start below, by either creating a new game by chosing from a couple different game modes, or join an already existing game

Gain points from each word you find, and the quicker you find each word, the more points you get. The catch though, is that once a player finds a word, that word is crossed off and no other player can find that word. Additionally, you gain more points the shorter the word is.
