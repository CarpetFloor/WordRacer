To enter a word, just type - you don't have to click on the input or anything.

Then hit enter to try a word.

You can use backspace to delete a character, or left arrow to clear the entire input

Words are case-insensitive, and will automatically get converted to uppercase.


Please note that input words can only have a max length of 15 letters, so if you are typing but nothing is happening, that might be why.
